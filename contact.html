Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a Yorkie puppy?

Simply contact us via WhatsApp, select your preferred puppy from our available listings, and we'll guide you through the reservation process. A deposit is required to hold your puppy.

What delivery options do you offer?

We offer three delivery options: Ground transportation, flight nanny service, and air cargo delivery. All options are handled with the utmost care to ensure your puppy arrives safely and comfortably.

Are your puppies vaccinated and health-checked?

Yes! All our Yorkshire Terrier puppies come with up-to-date vaccinations, deworming, and a comprehensive health check by a licensed veterinarian. We also provide a 10-year health commitment.

Can I visit to see the puppies in person?

Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss visit arrangements. We can also provide live video calls so you can see your puppy before making a decision.

What is your refund/return policy?

We stand behind the health and quality of our puppies. If any health issues arise within the first 10 days, we offer a full refund or replacement. Please chat with us on WhatsApp for complete policy details.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for qualified buyers. Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss payment options that work best for you.
